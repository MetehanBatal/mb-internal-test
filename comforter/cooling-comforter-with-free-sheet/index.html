Frequently asked questions

What is the thread count of the Miracle Cooling Comforter?

The comforter is made with 300-thread count Miracle Clean & Cool™ fabrics. The thread count is used to determine the quality and not the thickness of the comforter. The high thread count in our comforters guarantees that they are softer than traditional comforters.

What is this comforter made of?

Our Cooling Comforter features a 90% poly 10% Spandex blend and a luxury 100% down alternative fiber fill.

What is Silvadur™ Antimicrobial Technology?

Silvadur™ Antimicrobial Technology provides odor control by preventing microbe-derived odor and absorbing common causes of odor to mitigate unpleasant scents. It also controls odor-causing microorganisms that can accumulate on fabrics from skin contact and laundering. You can feel safe sleeping on it every night as it’s dermatologically tested for skin tolerance.

What’s Q-max?

Q-max measures how cool a fabric feels, and with the Cooling Comforter's Q-max score of 0.4+, you stay cooler at night without any added effort.

How often do I have to wash my Cooling Comforter?

Regular comforters need to be washed an average of once per month, however, doing so on a regular basis can be time-consuming for most people. Miracle’s antibacterial comforters only need to be washed three to four times a year, saving you time, helping to keep your skin clear, and your bedding odor-free!

What tests have you done to ensure your comforter is effective in eliminating bacteria?

We hired an independent agency whose mission is to test the transmission of infections in the products they serve. The scientists in this agency have over 20 years of academic research experience and have worked on numerous research initiatives including the use of antimicrobial surfaces in healthcare environments. The lab tests products that reduce bacteria on environmental surfaces including soft linen goods. We tested multiple products with and without silver-infused technology and noticed that all products without our unique silver-infused technology doubled in bacteria every 20 minutes. All products with our silver-infused technology reduced bacteria growth by up to 99.7%.
