What are the products made of?

Miracle Made® Sheets are made from our innovative Miracle Clean & Cool™ fabrics which are NASA-inspired and silver-infused. Generation 1 of the product is made from premium long-staple cotton. Generation 2 of the product is made from premium Eucalyptus Lyocell (51%) & Polyester (49%) blend. Generation 3 is made with premium long-staple cotton. The Extra Luxe sheets are made with COOLIT fabric, enhancing the self-cooling properties of the sheets. The majority of Miracle Made® Sheets shipping in 2024 are made from the Generation 3 product, though some colors still ship in Generation 1 or 2. All generations of the product feel luxuriously soft and are infused with silver.

How often do they have to be washed compared to regular sheets?

Regular sheets typically are recommended to be washed weekly. However, doing so on a regular basis can be time-consuming for most people. That's why we offer bed sheets that prevent up to 99.7% of bacteria growth so they can be washed up to an estimated 3x times less than traditional sheets, helping you save time and keep clean!

How does silver help protect us?

Silver is scientifically proven to help prevent bacterial growth. By infusing silver into our fabrics, our sheets are specially designed to help keep you clean and safe.

How does silver protect us?

Some metals like silver are scientifically proven to repel microbes and bacteria. The silver in our sheets is specially designed to keep you clean and safe.

What is the thread count?

The most recent generations of Miracle Made® Sheets are made with 300-thread-count fabrics to help give a premium and silky-soft feeling. Beware! Many brands selling sheets claiming a 1,000+ thread count are bending the truth and simply cutting the threads in their product to increase their thread count artificially. Does it have strong chemicals?
